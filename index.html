Our Story

Our love story began at our meeting at Chowking Unciano on August 2017. We became friends. We got to know each other more. Eventually, we became a couple after six months. We met with our respective families. For our first year, we traveled together and ate a lot in places with magnificent views. For our succeeding years, we still travel and eat out a lot. We serve the church together through music. Finally, we got engaged on April 5, 2022. Now, we are going to face the next chapter of our lives together through marriage.
